# Payment Practices

Business Central extension for calculating and reporting payment times for vendors and customers.

## Overview

This app enables the calculation of payment times aggregated across various metrics, helping meet regulatory requirements for payment time reporting.

## Structure

- `src/Tables/` - Data tables
- `src/Pages/` - UI pages
- `src/Reports/` - Reports
- `src/Core/` - Business logic, codeunits, permissions
